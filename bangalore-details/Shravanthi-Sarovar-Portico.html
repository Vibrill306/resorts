Let Shravanthi Sarovar Portico
be your luxurious stay.

Plunge into the gleaming beauty of Bangalore, the startup and tech hub of India with Shravanthi Sarovar Portico, our customer appointment is meant to make you feel at home while you are away from home. The rustic yet classy interior of the hotel is adorned with beautiful artworks and valuable antiques. This 4 star hotel in Bangalore is in close proximity to various tourist spots making it easier for you to explore more of its wonders.

Explore The Places Near Shravanthi Sarovar Portico

Strategically located at the heart of Bangalore this beautiful resort is at a distance of 18 min from the Jayprakash metro station and 34 Km from the Kempegowda airport. Some of the places near this stunning resort are Lal Bagh Botanical Garden, Cubbon Park, Bangalore Palace, ISKCON temple, Tippu Sultan Palace and Bannerghatta National Park.
